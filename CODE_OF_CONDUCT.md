# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Actaro welcoming and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of identity, background, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

Be respectful, constructive, empathetic, and focused on what is best for the community. Do not harass, insult, discriminate against, or expose private information about others.

Project maintainers may remove or reject contributions and temporarily or permanently ban participants for unacceptable behavior. Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the repository's available private contact channel. Maintainers will review reports fairly and protect reporter privacy where possible.
